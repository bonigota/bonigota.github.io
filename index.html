Projects

  • Mapart Machine

    Machine only works for carpet made flat maps.

    Each map takes around 1h15min to complete.

    The Litematica mod is required.

    It is designed for servers that do not allow bots such as baritone.

    Must be built far from snow.

    You can cover the platform with normal glass to protect yourself against Phantoms. Just make sure to not obstruct sweepers. Alternatively you can build it bellow sea level since Phantoms don't spawn there. I suggest building it on a mushroom island, so there will be no hostile mobs around you.

    If your server does not allow chunk loaders, 2 accounts are required. 1 to load the nether side, and 1 to place carpets.
